## 🖼️ 2. Graphic Assets & Prompts

You can use text-to-image AI tools (like Midjourney, DALL-E 3, or Stable Diffusion) to generate high-quality assets. Below are the specific prompts you can use.

**4K-first workflow (recommended):**
- Create master creatives at 3840 x 2160 (or higher) for best quality.
- Export final Play Console files in required sizes (icon 512x512, feature graphic 1024x500, screenshots 9:16).

### 🔹 App Icon (Use existing one or generate a new one)
- **Upload Size Required:** 512x512 pixels (PNG, no alpha/transparency for the edges).
- **Existing file location:** `ios/Runner/Assets.xcassets/AppIcon.appiconset/Icon-App-1024x1024@1x.png`
- **Generative AI Prompt:**
  > A premium, modern, and sleek app icon for an AI productivity app called "Synap". The design should feature a futuristic, minimalist brain or a glowing neural network node structure. deep space dark navy/black background with vibrant electric blue and cyan graphical elements. Clean, polished, no text, Apple iOS standard icon style.

### 🔹 Feature Graphic
- **Dimensions:** 1024 x 500 px (landscape)
- **Format:** JPEG or 24-bit PNG (NO transparency)
- **Generative AI Prompt:**
  > A stunning, ultra-high definition promotional banner (aspect ratio 2:1) for an AI application. The background is a futuristic dark interstellar space scene with subtle glowing nebula and floating energetic particles. On the right side, an abstract, glowing, cyan and purple neural network connecting. Left side should have empty negative space for text overlays. Cinematic lighting, highly detailed, premium tech vibe.

### 🔹 Screenshots Graphics / Mockup Backgrounds
Google Play allows you to frame your screenshots inside phone mockups with text above them. Instead of generating the screens from scratch, use an AI to generate an awesome background, then place a phone mockup over it using a tool like Figma or Canva.

- **Quantity:** Minimum 2, Maximum 8
- **Dimensions:** 1080x1920 px (portrait) recommended
- **Generative AI Prompt (For Screenshot Backgrounds):**
  > A vertical, abstract, stunning background image for a mobile app presentation. Deep space dark aesthetics with subtle gradients of glowing cyan, neon blue, and deep purple. Soft bokeh light effects and a clean tech vibe. Ensure the center and lower areas are relatively clean so a smartphone mockup can be placed over it. The top area should accommodate bold white text. 8k, photorealistic quality.

**How to make the final screenshots (Best Method):**
1. Take real screenshots from your phone (or emulator) showing:
   - Voice Hub screen
   - Tool directory grid
   - Specific tool detail or category
   - Premium upgrade screen
2. Use Canva or Figma.
3. Put the AI-generated abstract background behind.
4. Place a standard Phone Frame (like iPhone 15 or Pixel 8) in the middle.
5. Put your real screenshot inside the frame.
6. Add bold, white text at the top (e.g., "Discover 1200+ AI Tools", "Talk to an AI Assistant").
