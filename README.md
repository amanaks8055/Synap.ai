# Synap – The Premium AI Ecosystem

Synap is a next-generation AI companion designed to help users discover, track, and master the world of AI tools while maximizing their free-tier limits.

## 🚀 Vision
In a world with hundreds of AI tools, Synap acts as your central command center—helping you navigate through ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and more with a premium, fluid, and high-performance interface.

## ✨ Core Features

### 1. Mobile Hub (Flutter)
*   **Curated Discovery**: 200+ AI tools categorized for productivity, creation, and coding.
*   **Voice Hub**: High-speed, intuitive voice interface for seamless AI interaction.
*   **Elite UI/UX**: A "Dark Void" aesthetic featuring glassomorphism, aurora backgrounds, and micro-animations.
*   **Real-time Sync**: Your data follows you across mobile and web.

### 2. Smart Tracker (Chrome Extension)
*   **Usage Monitoring**: Automatically tracks prompts on ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini.
*   **Limit Alerts**: Get notified when you reach 80% and 100% of your free-tier limits.
*   **Intelligent Switching**: Recommends alternative tools when your primary AI's limits are exhausted.

### 3. Unified Backend (Supabase)
*   **Secure Auth**: Native Google and Apple sign-in.
*   **Cloud Sync**: Favorites and usage metadata synced across all platforms.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
*   **Frontend**: Flutter (Mobile), HTML/CSS/JS (Chrome Extension)
*   **Backend**: Supabase (PostgreSQL, Auth, Edge Functions)
*   **State Management**: Flutter Bloc
*   **Animations**: Rive & Custom Canvas Painters
