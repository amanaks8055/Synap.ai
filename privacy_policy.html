Privacy Policy for Synap

Last Updated: March 6, 2026

Synap ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use the Synap mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

User Authentication Data: When you sign in using Google or Email, we collect your name, email address, and profile picture URL. This data is used to create and manage your personal account.

Voice Interaction Data: When you use the Voice Hub, your audio is processed for speech-to-text conversion. We do not store original audio recordings on our servers; only the resulting text queries may be processed to provide AI responses.

Usage Data: We may collect information on how the app is accessed and used, such as tool discovery history, favorite tools, and feature engagement. This helps us improve Synap for you.

2. How We Store Your Data

Supabase: Your profile information, preferences, and favorites are securely stored using Supabase, our backend service provider. This data is stored in encrypted databases to ensure privacy and security.

Local Storage: Some preferences (like guest favorites and theme settings) are stored locally on your device for a faster user experience.

3. Third-Party Services

AdMob: Synap uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data (such as your device's advertising ID) to show personalized ads. You can manage these settings in your Google account.

Google Sign-In: We use Google services for authentication. Their privacy policy applies to the information they provide to us during the sign-in process.

4. Data Retention

We retain your user profile and favorites for as long as your account is active. You may request the deletion of your account and associated data at any time through the app settings or by contacting us.

5. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
